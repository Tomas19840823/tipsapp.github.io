What is TipsApp?

We bridge the gap between grateful customers and hardworking professionals. In a cashless world, saying "Thank you" shouldn't be hard.

Direct Payments

Tips go directly from your bank account to the Pro's account. No middlemen holding your money.

Interactive Map

Find nearby waiters, bartenders, and service staff instantly using our geolocation map.

Transparent History

Track who you've tipped or how much you've earned with clear, date-filtered transaction history.

How to Send a Tip

Leaving a tip takes seconds. No cash? No problem.

1. Find a Pro

Open the app to see nearby service professionals on the map or search by name.

2. Choose Amount

Tap on a profile, view their details, and select the amount you wish to tip.

3. Secure Payment

Select your bank via Paysera. The transfer is instant and secure.
