How to Send a Tip

Leaving a tip takes seconds.

1. Find a Pro

Open the app to see nearby service professionals on the map or search by name or location.

2. Choose Amount

Tap on a profile (orange dot), select the amount you wish to tip. Also rate and provide comment. You can do it being as a Guest or registered user.

3. Secure Payment

Select your bank. The transfer is instant and secure.
